The crow of a rooster awakens you from slumber, followed by the sound of a brass band cranking up. "Roll up! Roll up for the Pudding Faire!” cries a voice from the street below. Peering out the inn’s window, you see a crowd of happy halflings and gnomes bustling toward a fairground on the village green. It is seven o’clock in he morning on the day of the annual Honeypuddle Pudding Faire!
